Activity 2 Page for Luis Vega Mendez




Luis is a student of Management Information Systems at the University of Nebraska at Omaha in Omaha Nebraska. He has over 20 years of Information Systems experience from the United States Air Force, were he worked as a Airborne Mission Systems Specialist on board multiple aircrafts. He was a Communications Systems Technician on board the E-8C J-STARS for six years while stationed at Robins Air Force Base in Warner Robins Georgia. For five years after that, he was a Communications Operator on board the E-3A NATO AWACS while stationed at Geilenkirchen NATO Air Base in Geilenkirchen Germany. Finally, he was a Radio and Voice Operator on board the E-4B NAOC for 10 years while stationed at Offutt Air Force Base in Bellevue Nebraska. Currently he is raising his four kids while attending the University of Nebraska Omaha full-time, and using his VA educational benefits and military retirement as his source of income.
