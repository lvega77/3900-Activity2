E-8C J-STARS (Joint Surveilance Tactical Attack Radar System)

Luis was a Communications System Technitian on board the E-8C J-STARS for six years. He became an instructor and an evaluator, and helped train new airmen as they journeyed to learn the operations of this complex technological marvel.

Learn more about J-STARS

E-3A NATO AWACS (North Atlantic Treaty Organization Airborne Warning And Control System)

Luis was a Communications Operator on board the E-3A NATO AWACS for five years. He worked as one of many US representatives in this multi-national coallition known as NATO.

Learn more about NATO AWACS

E-4B NAOC (National Airborne Operations Center)

Luis was a Radio and Voice Operator on board the E-4B NAOC for ten years. He was part of the 24/7 alert crew responsible for many aspects of the day to day protection of the United States. During this time he was the Superintendent of the Training Squadron, the Squadron's COMSEC Responsible Officer, and the Communications Scheduler between many other hats he wore.
