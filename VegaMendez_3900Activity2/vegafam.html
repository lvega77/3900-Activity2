The Vega Family

This is Luis' family. From left to right: Adriana, Iliana, Luis, Joseph, and Jacob.

Luis' family is composed of two teenage girls, two younger boys, and himself. The girls and the boys have different mothers, with whom he works together to share the responsibility of raising the kids. Luis and his kids are currently living together at their humble home in a neighborhood called "Quail Creek" in Bellevue Nebraska.
